Usabilathon 2025: Workiva AI Anomaly Detection

A user-centered approach to explainable AI in spreadsheets, grounded in HCI research.

Problem Statement

Spreadsheet users across industries rely heavily on manual error detection, intuition, and formula-based validation. However, these methods break down under time pressure, data complexity, or scale. Despite advances in AI, many users distrust automated tools, specially when they lack transparency or control. The challenge: how might we introduce explainable, non-intrusive AI assistance into a spreadsheet workflow without disrupting user autonomy or increasing cognitive load?

Discover

We began by understanding how real users identify spreadsheet anomalies. Through interviews, we learned that users:

  • Rely on intuition and team cross-checks
  • Rarely use automated anomaly detection tools
  • Prioritize control and trust over automation

“We don’t use tools to identify patterns — we are the tools.”

– Interview participant, Data Analyst

“Sometimes I export the data to a separate sheet just to test formulas cleanly.”

– Interview participant, Finance Lead

Define

We synthesized our findings into themes: trust, control, and cognitive overload. From these, we shaped “How Might We” questions:

  • How might we highlight anomalies without overwhelming users?
  • How might we give users control to validate or dismiss AI suggestions?

“I don’t want AI making decisions for me—I want it to point me in the right direction.”

– Luke Harper, Sales Rep Persona

Affinity map summarizing key themes from interviews

Design

We designed a transparent AI assistant with key features:

  • Optional AI Scan: Users choose when to run it
  • In-cell alerts: Non-intrusive flags with visual cues
  • Dismiss or explore: Full user control
View Full Prototype

Deliver

We delivered a refined Figma prototype and documentation highlighting usability and trust. Key benefits included:

  • Improved anomaly awareness through visual aids
  • Reduced cognitive load using plain language summaries
  • Empowered users to override AI suggestions confidently
