petsARus: AR Pet Game for iOS & Android

petsARus is a mobile AR game inspired by Nintendogs and Tamagotchi, where players care for digital pets in the real world using voice commands, touch interactions, and immersive animations. Designed to provide fun, comfort, and companionship anytime, anywhere.

This was a team project by David Tang, Nick Menough, and Lenet Ron, blending game design, Unity development, and UX research to create a delightful AR experience.

Download Project PDF

What Makes petsARus Special?

  • Choose starter pets: Iguana, Pug, German Shepherd, or Deer
  • Use voice commands like "Sit", "Wiggle Tail", "Lay Down" with Google Speech-to-Text
  • Touch toys and treats to interact with pets in real-time
  • Fill affection meter to unlock confetti celebration and bond feedback
  • Built for both Android and iOS using Unity & Vuforia
  • Future: multiplayer pet park, social trading, and in-game economy
  • Pet cosmetic customization with coins and collectibles
  • Expandable modular system for events and updates

Follow your pawprints to a world where pets play back.
